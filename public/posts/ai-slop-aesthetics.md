The web is full of templates that all look like cousins. Clean? Yes. Memorable? Not really.

## Why the sameness happens

Most auto-generated UIs optimize for *passing* design, not distinct design. You get predictable spacing, predictable color ramps, and predictable hierarchy.

## The antidote

Pick one dominant idea and over-commit to it:

- A single visual motif
- A deliberate type pairing
- One strong accent color with discipline
- Motion used for storytelling, not decoration

## Practical rule

If your landing page can be swapped with someone else's by replacing logo and text, your design system still needs personality.
